Refund Policy

You can request a refund four (4) weeks prior to the event.

Deadline for the Digital Summit in Tampa is February 23, 2027. You can transfer your pass to another person and/or event at any time up until the day before event March 23, 2027. Please note that transferred passes from one event to another, are not eligible for refunds.

Passes may only be transferred from one Digital Summit event to another one (1) time. Once a pass has been transferred to a different event, it is not eligible for any additional event transfers.

Transferred passes must be redeemed within one (1) calendar year of the original event dates. After that time, passes become non-transferable and expire.

“No-shows” are not eligible for partial or full credit after the event. Service and processing fees are non-refundable.

If you have any questions, please contact registration@digitalsummit.com or call 919-529-5373.

Parking & Locating Digital Summit

Self-parking is NOT available at the Hilton Tampa Downtown.


Valet parking is available. Digital Summit does NOT validate attendee parking.

These additional parking options were built using AI, we recommend you confirm the hours of these lots prior to your arrival and/or contact the hotel directly with questions.


1. Fort Brooke Garage
Location: 107 N Franklin St, Tampa, FL 33602
Distance: About a 5-minute walk from Hilton Tampa Downtown.
Rates: Typically around $1.60 per hour, with a daily maximum of about $9-$12.
Details: This garage offers a large number of spaces and is often a go-to for downtown Tampa visitors.


2. Tampa Convention Center Garage
Location: 333 S Franklin St, Tampa, FL 33602
Distance: About a 10-minute walk from the hotel.
Rates: Approximately $2.00 per hour, with a daily maximum of $10-$12.
Details: Convenient for those attending downtown events, with easy access to the Riverwalk.


3. Poe Garage
Location: 802 N Ashley Dr, Tampa, FL 33602
Distance: About a 10-12 minute walk from the Hilton.
Rates: Around $2.00 per hour, with daily rates up to $12.
Details: Located near the Tampa Museum of Art and Curtis Hixon Park, this garage is slightly farther but a good option for cheaper parking.


4. Street Parking
Availability: There is metered parking available on nearby streets, such as E Kennedy Blvd and N Franklin St.
Rates: Meters typically charge $2.50 per hour, and some offer free parking on weekends and after 6 PM on weekdays.


5. Private Parking Lots
There are smaller private lots near the hotel, which often charge flat daily rates between $5 and $15. These lots typically cater to office workers during the day but may offer better availability in the evenings and weekends.


Parking Apps:
You can also use parking apps like ParkMobile or SpotHero to reserve a spot in advance in nearby garages or lots, which can sometimes offer discounts compared to paying on-site.
