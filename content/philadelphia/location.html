Getting there

Traveling from out of town? Make sure you use Digital Summit’s exclusive HotelMap, which gives our attendees access to discounted rates in Philadelphia.

These directions are built using AI, we recommend you rely on your personal phone or vehicle GPS for more detailed driving directions.
From Philadelphia International Airport (PHL)

  • Exit the airport and follow signs for I-95 North toward Philadelphia.
  • Take Exit 17 for I-76 East / Center City.
  • Continue on I-76 East and follow signs for Central Philadelphia / 30th Street.
  • Take the exit for South Street / University Avenue.
  • Continue straight, then turn left onto Broad Street (Route 611 North).
  • Drive north on Broad Street for approximately 1 mile.
  • The Bellevue Hotel will be on your right, at the corner of Broad and Walnut Streets.


From Center City Philadelphia

  • Navigate to Broad Street (Route 611).
  • Head south on Broad Street toward Walnut Street.
  • The Bellevue Hotel is located at 200 S Broad Street, on the west side of the street, just past Walnut Street.
  • The hotel entrance is directly on Broad Street.

From I-95 North or South

  • Take I-95 to Exit 17 for I-76 East / Center City.
  • Follow signs for Central Philadelphia.
  • Take the exit for South Street / University Avenue.
  • Continue straight and turn left onto Broad Street (Route 611 North).
  • Drive north on Broad Street for approximately 1 mile.
  • The Bellevue Hotel will be on your right, at Broad and Walnut Streets.

From New Jersey via the Walt Whitman Bridge

  • Cross the Walt Whitman Bridge into Philadelphia.
  • Follow signs for I-76 East / Center City.
  • Take the exit for South Street / University Avenue.
  • Continue straight and turn left onto Broad Street (Route 611 North).
  • Drive north on Broad Street for approximately 1 mile.
  • The Bellevue Hotel will be on your right, at the corner of Broad and Walnut Streets.

By Subway (SEPTA Broad Street Line)

  • Take the Broad Street Line to Walnut–Locust Station.
  • Exit the station and walk south on Broad Street.
  • The Bellevue Hotel is one block south, on the west side of Broad Street.

From Philadelphia International Airport (PHL)

  • Take the Airport Line train to Suburban Station.
  • From Suburban Station, walk south to Broad Street or transfer to the Broad Street Line (southbound).
  • Exit at Walnut–Locust Station and walk one block south to the hotel.

Tip: The hotel is located directly on Broad Street, making it easily accessible from multiple SEPTA routes.

The Bellevue Hotel is easily accessible by bike via Center City bike lanes. Ride toward Broad Street, head south to Walnut Street, and the hotel will be on the west side of Broad, just past Walnut.
