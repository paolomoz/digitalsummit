Refund Policy

Refund requests must be submitted at least four (4) weeks prior to the event.

The refund deadline for Digital Summit Philadelphia 2026 is August 26, 2026. Passes may be transferred to another person and/or another Digital Summit event at any time up until the day before the event, September 22, 2026. Please note that transferred passes from one event to another, are not eligible for refunds.

Passes may only be transferred from one Digital Summit event to another one (1) time. Once a pass has been transferred to a different event, it is not eligible for any additional event transfers.

Transferred passes must be redeemed within one (1) calendar year of the original event dates. After that time, passes become non-transferable and expire.

“No-shows” are not eligible for partial or full credit after the event. Service and processing fees are non-refundable.

If you have any questions, please contact registration@digitalsummit.com or call 919-529-5373.

Special Requests/assistance

If you require special assistance (dietary requests, nursing moms, accessibility, allergies, etc.), please contact registration@digitalsummit.com to provide advance notice by September 2, 2026.

Requests made after this date may not be accommodated.

What to wear

You'll find a range of attire at the conference but generally most attendees are in business casual.

Temperatures in conference halls may fluctuate widely. It’s advised to dress in layers and wear comfortable shoes.

Getting to The Belleview Hotel & Digital Summit

Directions

These directions are built using AI, we recommend you rely on your personal phone or vehicle GPS for more detailed driving directions.


From Philadelphia International Airport (PHL)

  • Exit the airport and follow signs for I-95 North toward Philadelphia.
  • Take Exit 17 for I-76 East / Center City.
  • Continue on I-76 East and follow signs for Central Philadelphia / 30th Street.
  • Take the exit for South Street / University Avenue.
  • Continue straight, then turn left onto Broad Street (Route 611 North).
  • Drive north on Broad Street for approximately 1 mile.
  • The Bellevue Hotel will be on your right, at the corner of Broad and Walnut Streets.


From Center City Philadelphia

  • Navigate to Broad Street (Route 611).
  • Head south on Broad Street toward Walnut Street.
  • The Bellevue Hotel is located at 200 S Broad Street, on the west side of the street, just past Walnut Street.
  • The hotel entrance is directly on Broad Street.

From I-95 North or South

  • Take I-95 to Exit 17 for I-76 East / Center City.
  • Follow signs for Central Philadelphia.
  • Take the exit for South Street / University Avenue.
  • Continue straight and turn left onto Broad Street (Route 611 North).
  • Drive north on Broad Street for approximately 1 mile.
  • The Bellevue Hotel will be on your right, at Broad and Walnut Streets.

From New Jersey via the Walt Whitman Bridge

  • Cross the Walt Whitman Bridge into Philadelphia.
  • Follow signs for I-76 East / Center City.
  • Take the exit for South Street / University Avenue.
  • Continue straight and turn left onto Broad Street (Route 611 North).
  • Drive north on Broad Street for approximately 1 mile.
  • The Bellevue Hotel will be on your right, at the corner of Broad and Walnut Streets.
