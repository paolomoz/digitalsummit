Getting to the Colorado Convention Center & Digital Summit

Directions

These directions are built using AI, we recommend you rely on your personal phone or vehicle GPS for more detailed driving directions.

From Denver International Airport (DEN):

  • Exit the airport and merge onto Pena Boulevard.
  • Continue on Pena Boulevard and take the exit for I-70 West.
  • Merge onto I-70 West and proceed for approximately 18 miles.
  • Take exit 274 for I-25 South toward Colorado Springs.
  • Merge onto I-25 South and continue for about 2 miles.
  • Take exit 210A for Auraria Parkway.
  • Merge onto Auraria Parkway and continue as it becomes Speer Boulevard.
  • Turn left onto Stout Street.
  • Turn right onto 14th Street.
  • The Convention Center will be on your right.

From the North (e.g., Boulder):

  • Take US-36 East toward Denver.
  • Merge onto I-25 South.
  • Follow steps 6-10 from the directions above.

From the South (e.g., Colorado Springs):

  • Take I-25 North toward Denver.
  • Take exit 210B for Colfax Avenue.
  • Merge onto Colfax Avenue heading east.
  • Turn right onto Speer Boulevard.
  • Turn left onto Stout Street.
  • Turn right onto 14th Street.
  • The Convention Center will be on your right.

From the West (e.g., Golden):

  • Take US-6 East toward Denver.
  • Merge onto I-25 North.
  • Follow steps 2-7 from the "From the South" directions above.
