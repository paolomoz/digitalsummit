Refund Policy

Refund requests must be submitted at least four (4) weeks prior to the event.

The refund deadline for Digital Summit Denver 2026 is May 18, 2026. Passes may be transferred to another person and/or another Digital Summit event at any time up until the day before the event, June 14, 2026. Please note that transferred passes from one event to another, are not eligible for refunds.

Passes may only be transferred from one Digital Summit event to another one (1) time. Once a pass has been transferred to a different event, it is not eligible for any additional event transfers.

Transferred passes must be redeemed within one (1) calendar year of the original event dates. After that time, passes become non-transferable and expire.

“No-shows” are not eligible for partial or full credit after the event. Service and processing fees are non-refundable.

If you have any questions, please contact registration@digitalsummit.com or call 919-529-5373.

Getting to the Colorado Convention Center & Digital Summit

Directions

These directions are built using AI, we recommend you rely on your personal phone or vehicle GPS for more detailed driving directions.

From Denver International Airport (DEN):

  • Exit the airport and merge onto Pena Boulevard.
  • Continue on Pena Boulevard and take the exit for I-70 West.
  • Merge onto I-70 West and proceed for approximately 18 miles.
  • Take exit 274 for I-25 South toward Colorado Springs.
  • Merge onto I-25 South and continue for about 2 miles.
  • Take exit 210A for Auraria Parkway.
  • Merge onto Auraria Parkway and continue as it becomes Speer Boulevard.
  • Turn left onto Stout Street.
  • Turn right onto 14th Street.
  • The Convention Center will be on your right.

From the North (e.g., Boulder):

  • Take US-36 East toward Denver.
  • Merge onto I-25 South.
  • Follow steps 6-10 from the directions above.

From the South (e.g., Colorado Springs):

  • Take I-25 North toward Denver.
  • Take exit 210B for Colfax Avenue.
  • Merge onto Colfax Avenue heading east.
  • Turn right onto Speer Boulevard.
  • Turn left onto Stout Street.
  • Turn right onto 14th Street.
  • The Convention Center will be on your right.

From the West (e.g., Golden):

  • Take US-6 East toward Denver.
  • Merge onto I-25 North.
  • Follow steps 2-7 from the "From the South" directions above.

Parking & Locating Digital Summit

Digital Summit does not validate parking.

The Colorado Convention Center provides access to a 665 space onsite covered parking garage but currently capacity has been reduced due to the ongoing expansion. The garage provides access to the Colorado Convention Center and the Denver Performing Arts Complex (DPAC).

The garage is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to ALL visitors and guests attending ANY event or business in the downtown area. If you are coming to a scheduled event in our facility, DPAC or any venue downtown, please arrive early and remember to use our quick and easy automated pay stations. We offer contactless payment through text or QR code. Once you have parked walk up to any pay machine and scan the QR code or use the text prompt to purchase parking.

View website for up to date parking fee information.
