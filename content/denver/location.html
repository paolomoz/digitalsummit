Getting there

Driving Directions

These directions are built using AI, we recommend you rely on your personal phone or vehicle GPS for more detailed driving directions.

From Denver International Airport (DEN):

  1. Exit the airport and merge onto Pena Boulevard.
  2. Continue on Pena Boulevard and take the exit for I-70 West.
  3. Merge onto I-70 West and proceed for approximately 18 miles.
  4. Take exit 274 for I-25 South toward Colorado Springs.
  5. Merge onto I-25 South and continue for about 2 miles.
  6. Take exit 210A for Auraria Parkway.
  7. Merge onto Auraria Parkway and continue as it becomes Speer Boulevard.
  8. Turn left onto Stout Street.
  9. Turn right onto 14th Street.
  10. The Convention Center will be on your right.

From the North (e.g., Boulder): Take US-36 East toward Denver, merge onto I-25 South, then follow steps 6–10 above.

From the South (e.g., Colorado Springs): Take I-25 North toward Denver, take exit 210B for Colfax Avenue, merge onto Colfax Avenue heading east, turn right onto Speer Boulevard, left onto Stout Street, right onto 14th Street. The Convention Center will be on your right.

From the West (e.g., Golden): Take US-6 East toward Denver, merge onto I-25 North, then follow the “From the South” steps above.

Parking Information

Digital Summit does not validate parking.

The Colorado Convention Center provides access to a 665 space onsite covered parking garage but currently capacity has been reduced due to the ongoing expansion. The garage provides access to the Colorado Convention Center and the Denver Performing Arts Complex (DPAC).

The garage is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to ALL visitors and guests attending ANY event or business in the downtown area. If you are coming to a scheduled event in our facility, DPAC or any venue downtown, please arrive early and remember to use the quick and easy automated pay stations.

View website for up to date parking fee information.

Public Transportation

Due to ongoing construction, the Theatre District/Convention Center Station is currently closed. Alternative public transit options:

  • Light Rail: D and H Lines are rerouted to Union Station during the construction period. From Union Station, take the Free MallRide shuttle to California St and 17th St, then walk south to the Convention Center.
  • Bus Services: Several bus routes provide service to the area, including Routes 0, 0L, 6, 9, 10, 15, 19, 28, and 52. Use the RTD Trip Planner to determine the best route from your location.
  • Real-Time Updates: Check the RTD Service Alerts for the latest information on transit services.

Bicycle and Motorcycle

Denver offers a comprehensive network of bike-friendly routes to facilitate your journey to the Convention Center.

  • Denver Bike Map: Utilize the Denver Bike Map to identify dedicated bike lanes, shared roadways, and multi-use trails.
  • Google Maps: Activate the “Bicycling” layer on Google Maps to view bike-friendly routes.
  • Cherry Creek Trail: A scenic, off-street path running alongside Cherry Creek. Exit the trail at Speer Boulevard and continue north to 14th Street.
  • South Platte River Trail: An extensive trail following the South Platte River. Exit at Confluence Park, proceed east on 15th Street, then right onto Welton Street, and left onto 14th Street.
  • Bicycle Parking: The Convention Center provides bicycle racks near the main entrances on 14th Street between Stout and California Streets.
  • Safety: Always wear a helmet, adhere to all traffic signals and signs, and use lights and reflective gear during low-light conditions.
