Refund Policy

Refund requests must be submitted at least four (4) weeks prior to the event.

The refund deadline for Digital Summit Atlanta 2026 is September 8, 2026. Passes may be transferred to another person and/or another Digital Summit event at any time up until the day before the event, October 5, 2026. Please note that transferred passes from one event to another, are not eligible for refunds.

Passes may only be transferred from one Digital Summit event to another one (1) time. Once a pass has been transferred to a different event, it is not eligible for any additional event transfers.

Transferred passes must be redeemed within one (1) calendar year of the original event dates. After that time, passes become non-transferable and expire.

“No-shows” are not eligible for partial or full credit after the event. Service and processing fees are non-refundable.

If you have any questions, please contact registration@digitalsummit.com or call 919-529-5373.

Special Requests/assistance

If you require special assistance (dietary requests, nursing moms, accessibility, allergies, etc.), please contact registration@digitalsummit.com to provide advance notice by Tuesday, September 15, 2026.

Requests made after this date may not be accommodated.

What to wear

You'll find a range of attire at the conference but generally most attendees are in business casual.

Temperatures in conference halls may fluctuate widely. It’s advised to dress in layers and wear comfortable shoes.

Getting to the Atlanta Marriott Marquis & Digital Summit

Directions

These directions are built using AI, we recommend you rely on your personal phone or vehicle GPS for more detailed driving directions.

From Hartsfield–Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL)

  • Exit the airport and follow signs for I-85 North / Downtown Atlanta.

  • Continue on I-85 North toward downtown.

  • Take the exit for GA-10 / Andrew Young International Blvd NE.

  • Turn left onto Andrew Young International Blvd NE.

  • Continue to Peachtree Center Avenue NE.

  • Turn right onto Peachtree Center Avenue NE — the hotel will be ahead on your left.


From Downtown Atlanta

  • Head toward Peachtree Street NE, one of the main north–south routes through downtown.

  • Turn onto Peachtree Center Avenue NE.

  • Continue for a short block — the hotel is on the left at 265 Peachtree Center Avenue NE (near other major downtown destinations

Parking & Locating Digital Summit

Digital Summit does not validate parking.

On-site and valet parking options are available (fees may apply).

Several public parking garages are adjacent to and near the hotel.


You've arrived at Digital Summit! Now what?

Registration

Registration kicks off at 8:00am. No need to print anything ahead of time! Just stop by our self-serve kiosk on site and print your badge instantly.

Once at the kiosk, search for your name or talk with our team to assist with your badge print.

Coffee

Coffee is available at any time in our sponsor area. Grab a cup of coffee and get to networking!

Partner Passport

Chat with all of our exhibitors and receive a sticker on your Partner Passport. Visit them all, return your card to registration before 2:00 pm on either day to be entered to win 1 of 4 insane prizes! Must be present to claim your prize.
