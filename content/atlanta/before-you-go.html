From Hartsfield–Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL)

  • Exit the airport and follow signs for I-85 North / Downtown Atlanta.

  • Continue on I-85 North toward downtown.

  • Take the exit for GA-10 / Andrew Young International Blvd NE.

  • Turn left onto Andrew Young International Blvd NE.

  • Continue to Peachtree Center Avenue NE.

  • Turn right onto Peachtree Center Avenue NE — the hotel will be ahead on your left.

  • Head toward Peachtree Street NE, one of the main north–south routes through downtown.
