  • Exit the airport and follow signs for I-85 North / Downtown Atlanta.

  • Continue on I-85 North toward downtown.

  • Take the exit for GA-10 / Andrew Young International Blvd NE.

  • Turn left onto Andrew Young International Blvd NE.

  • Continue to Peachtree Center Avenue NE.

  • Turn right onto Peachtree Center Avenue NE — the hotel will be ahead on your left.

  • Head toward Peachtree Street NE, one of the main north–south routes through downtown.

The Atlanta Marriott Marquis is easily accessible by bike from downtown. Ride toward Peachtree Street NE, then turn onto Peachtree Center Avenue NE. The hotel is located at the corner of Peachtree Center Ave NE and John Portman Blvd, with access via downtown bike lanes. Limited bike parking is available nearby; check with hotel staff for the closest racks.

Digital Summit does not validate parking.

On-site and valet parking options are available (fees may apply).

Several public parking garages are adjacent to and near the hotel.

By MARTA Rail

  • Take MARTA to Peachtree Center Station (Red or Gold Line).

  • Exit the station and follow signs for Peachtree Center.

  • Walk 2–3 minutes to the hotel via Peachtree Center Avenue.


From Hartsfield–Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL)

  • Take the MARTA Red or Gold Line (northbound) directly from the airport.

  • Exit at Peachtree Center Station.

  • Follow pedestrian signage to the Atlanta Marriott Marquis (approx. 2–3 minute walk).
