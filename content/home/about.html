About Digital Summit

style
prose

Over the years, Digital Summit has become the go-to resource for those seeking to further their knowledge and skill set in the ever-evolving digital ecosystem. Digital Summit produces over 1,200+ sessions, workshops, and seminars annually covering digital marketing best practices & tactics. All of this premium content is archived and available on-demand at Marketing Resources for our community.

Digital Summit keynote speakers have included luminaries like Seth Godin, Reshma Saujani, Mark Cuban, Randi Zuckerberg, Steve Wozniak, Ann Handley, Dan Pink, and Gary Vaynerchuk. Thought leaders from leading global brands like Disney, Microsoft, Google, Nike, Amazon, Twitter, and Facebook are regular presenters. Agency experts, influencers, consultants, and industry analysts also shape the editorial lineup at Digital Summit.

These multi-day live conferences are sponsored by the premier Martech, Adtech and agency vendors showcasing solutions for social media, email, advertising, hosting, content management, analytics, branding, design, and more.

Digital Summit’s commitment to accessibility

Creating an inclusive event experience is a shared responsibility. Digital Summit complies with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and strives to provide reasonable accommodations to meet individual attendee needs. If you require any special accommodations to fully participate in the event, please contact us at registration@digitalsummit.com at least two weeks prior to the event. We will work diligently with our venues to provide the requested accommodations in a timely and effective manner. Together, we can ensure everyone enjoys the event to the fullest.
