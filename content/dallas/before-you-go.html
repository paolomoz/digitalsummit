Refund Policy

Refund requests must be submitted at least four (4) weeks prior to the event.

The refund deadline for Digital Summit Dallas 2026 is November 10, 2026. Passes may be transferred to another person and/or another Digital Summit event at any time up until the day before the event, December 7, 2026. Please note that transferred passes from one event to another, are not eligible for refunds.

Passes may only be transferred from one Digital Summit event to another one (1) time. Once a pass has been transferred to a different event, it is not eligible for any additional event transfers.

Transferred passes must be redeemed within one (1) calendar year of the original event dates. After that time, passes become non-transferable and expire.

“No-shows” are not eligible for partial or full credit after the event. Service and processing fees are non-refundable.

If you have any questions, please contact registration@digitalsummit.com or call 919-529-5373.

Special Requests/assistance

If you require special assistance (dietary requests, nursing moms, accessibility, allergies, etc.), please contact registration@digitalsummit.com to provide advance notice by November 17, 2026.

Requests made after this date may not be accommodated.

What to wear

You'll find a range of attire at the conference but generally most attendees are in business casual.

Temperatures in conference halls may fluctuate widely. It’s advised to dress in layers and wear comfortable shoes.

Parking & Locating Digital Summit

Digital Summit does not validate parking.

On-site self-parking and valet options are available at the hotel.

Registration kicks off at 8:00am. No need to print anything ahead of time! Just stop by our self-serve kiosk on site and print your badge instantly.

Coffee

Coffee is available at any time in our sponsor area. Grab a cup of coffee and get to networking!

Partner Passport
