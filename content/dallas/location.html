Getting there

Traveling from out of town? Make sure you use Digital Summit’s exclusive HotelMap, which gives our attendees access to discounted rates in Dallas.

From Dallas/Fort Worth International Airport (DFW)

  • Exit DFW and head east on TX-114 toward Dallas.
  • Continue on TX-114 E and merge onto I-35E South toward downtown Dallas.
  • Take the Exit for Reunion Blvd / Downtown Dallas.
  • Turn left onto Reunion Blvd — you’ll see Reunion Tower nearby, a recognizable downtown landmark.
  • Continue on Reunion Blvd E to 300 Reunion Blvd, where the Hyatt Regency Dallas is located on your right.

From Dallas Love Field (DAL)

  • Head east on Mockingbird Ln toward Dallas.
  • Take I-35E South toward downtown.
  • Follow signs for Reunion Blvd / Downtown Dallas.
  • Turn left onto Reunion Blvd.
  • Continue to 300 Reunion Blvd — the hotel will be on your right.

From Downtown Dallas

  • Navigate to I-35E South or Main Street / Lamar Street toward the Reunion District.
  • Head onto Reunion Blvd.
  • The hotel is at 300 Reunion Blvd E — you’ll see Reunion Tower adjacent and the building directly across from it.

By DART Rail (Light Rail)

  • Take the DART Rail (Red, Blue, or Green Line) to West End Station — this is the closest rail stop to the hotel.
  • From West End Station, it’s about a 4-minute walk to Hyatt Regency Dallas.

By Bus

  • Several DART bus routes serve stops within a short walk of the hotel, including buses such as 109, 145, 214, 224, 230, 383, and 9 depending on your starting point.
  • Moovit
  • Get off at stops near Elm @ Record or Houston @ Young streets — both are a few minutes’ walk from the hotel.

From Airports

From Dallas/Fort Worth International Airport (DFW):

  • Take the DART Rail Orange Line from DFW Airport Station toward downtown.
  • Transfer as needed to reach West End Station (about a 50-minute rail trip).

From Dallas Love Field (DAL):

  • Take DART Route 005 bus from Love Field to the Inwood/Love Field rail station, then board DART Rail to West End Station (total trip ~45–60 min).

Digital Summit does not validate parking.

On-site self-parking and valet options are available at the hotel.

The hotel is adjacent to Reunion Tower, which is near several public parking areas in the downtown district.

General Accessible Features

The hotel provides accessible routes from the public entrance to key areas such as the restaurant spaces, swimming pool, fitness center, business center, meeting rooms/ballrooms, and public restrooms. These areas are designed to be reachable via accessible paths.

Wheelchair access is available in common areas and public spaces.

Accessible Guest Rooms

Hyatt Regency Dallas offers accessible guest rooms equipped with features like grab bars, accessible bathrooms, and roll-in showers (where available). These rooms also include lowered light switches, accessible bathroom fixtures, and other features to support mobility needs.

Additional Accessibility Notes

Guests with mobility disabilities — including those who use wheelchairs — can request specific room features and arrangements when booking or by contacting the hotel directly.

For service animals, most hotels, including Hyatt Regency properties, typically welcome them in accordance with ADA guidelines; contact the hotel for details on policies and requirements.

Visit the hotel's parking and accessibility page for more information.
