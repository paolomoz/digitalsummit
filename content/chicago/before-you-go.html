Getting to the Marriott Marquis Chicago & Digital Summit

Directions

These directions are built using AI, we recommend you rely on your personal phone or vehicle GPS for more detailed driving directions.

From Downtown Chicago (Loop area):

  • Get on I-90/I-94 South (Dan Ryan Expressway) from Congress Pkwy.
  • Follow I-90/I-94 South for about 3–4 miles.
  • Take exit 51B toward 18th Street / McCormick Place.
  • Merge onto S Martin Luther King Dr.
  • Turn right onto S Prairie Ave.
  • The hotel will be on your right.

From O’Hare Airport:

  • Take I-190 E toward Chicago.
  • Merge onto I-90 E / I-94 E toward the city.
  • Take exit 51B toward 18th Street / McCormick Place.
  • Merge onto S Martin Luther King Dr.
  • Turn right onto S Prairie Ave.
  • Arrive at 2121 S Prairie Ave.

From Midway Airport:

  • Get on I-55 N from S Cicero Ave.
  • Merge onto I-90/I-94 N toward downtown.
  • Take exit 51B toward 18th Street / McCormick Place.
  • Merge onto S Martin Luther King Dr.
  • Turn right onto S Prairie Ave.
  • Arrive at Marriott Marquis Chicago.
