What is the refund and transfer policy?

Refund requests must be submitted at least four weeks before the event — the deadline for Digital Summit Minneapolis 2026 is July 15, 2026. Passes can be transferred to another person or another Digital Summit event any time up until the day before the event, August 11, 2026. A pass may be transferred between events only once, and transferred passes must be redeemed within one calendar year of the original event date. Service and processing fees are non-refundable. No-shows are not eligible for credit. For questions, contact registration@digitalsummit.com or (919) 529-5373.
